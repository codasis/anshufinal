How we started

From one shaper's garage to a small catalog

ASURFERS began as a side project: a friend who shaped boards, and another who'd spent a decade sewing travel packs for expedition trips. We never planned to become a big catalog. Instead, we kept the list short on purpose.

Every board and bag we stock is chosen in small runs. When a batch sells out, we don't rush a reorder just to keep the page full — we wait until the next one is worth putting our name on.
